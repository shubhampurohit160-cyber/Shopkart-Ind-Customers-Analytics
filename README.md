# ShopKart India – E-Commerce Customer Shopping Behavior & Revenue Analytics

## Project Overview
ShopKart India is an end-to-end Data Analytics portfolio project developed using Python, SQL, and Power BI.  
The project analyzes 5000+ realistic Indian e-commerce customer transactions to identify:

- Revenue trends
- Customer purchase behavior
- Repeat customer contribution
- Payment mode preferences
- Delivery performance impact
- Discount effectiveness
- Product category profitability

---

## Tech Stack Used
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
- SQL (MySQL)
- Power BI
- CSV Dataset Analytics

---

## Business Problem Statement
Indian e-commerce companies generate huge transactional data daily, but raw order records alone do not reveal actionable business intelligence.

This project transforms raw customer order data into:
- KPI reports
- customer retention insights
- regional sales performance
- satisfaction trend analysis
- business recommendations

---

## Key Analysis Performed
- Total Revenue & Average Order Value
- Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis
- Top Selling Product Categories
- State Wise and City Wise Sales
- Payment Method Preference Analysis
- Repeat vs New Customer Revenue Contribution
- Delivery Delay vs Customer Rating
- Discount vs Revenue Correlation
- Top Customer Spending Pattern

## Key Business Insights
- Electronics and Fashion drive highest revenue share
- UPI and COD dominate customer payments
- Repeat customers generate stronger average order value
- Delivery delays significantly impact customer ratings
- Metro cities contribute maximum order revenue
